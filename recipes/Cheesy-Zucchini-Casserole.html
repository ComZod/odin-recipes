Cheesy Zucchini Casserole

Description:

A Cheesy Zucchini Casserole is a savory and flavorful dish that utilizes fresh zucchini, often abundant during the summer months. It’s known for its comforting qualities and ability to be enjoyed as a main course or a delicious side dish.

Here's a breakdown of the typical characteristics of a cheesy zucchini casserole:

Key Ingredients: Fresh zucchini (often grated or sliced), cheese (like cheddar, mozzarella, Parmesan, or a combination), eggs (for binding), and seasonings (such as garlic, onion, herbs like oregano, thyme, and basil). Some recipes include breadcrumbs or panko for topping or incorporating within the casserole.

Texture: It typically features tender, yet not mushy, zucchini pieces enveloped in a creamy, cheesy base. Many recipes incorporate a crispy topping, often made with breadcrumbs or panko, for added texture contrast.

Flavor Profile: The casserole is characterized by its savory and cheesy taste, often enhanced by the addition of herbs and spices. The mild sweetness of the zucchini complements the richness of the cheese and the earthy notes of the seasonings.

Preparation: While there are variations, a common method involves preparing the zucchini (grating or slicing and often salting to remove excess moisture), mixing it with the other ingredients, and baking it until bubbly and golden brown. Some recipes may suggest briefly cooking the zucchini and/or onions and garlic before baking to enhance the flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices bread, cubed
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 2 cups cubed zucchini
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt or to taste
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place bread cubes in a medium bowl and pour melted butter over top. Add zucchini, onion, egg, and garlic salt; mix well.
  3. Transfer the mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish and top with cheese.
  4. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Uncover the dish and bake for another 30 minutes.
